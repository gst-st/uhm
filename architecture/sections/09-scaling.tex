\section{Expressiveness and scaling: the octonionic ladder}
\label{sec:scaling}

Section~\ref{sec:composition} established a wall: a single unified subject
composes no deeper than three levels ($\mathrm{SAD}_{\max}=3$). A wall provokes
the engineering question this section answers, non-speculatively and from the
theory alone: \emph{if depth is capped, how does power scale?} Two naive answers
are foreclosed at once. Deeper composition is impossible --- the fourth level
requires purity $P>1$. And a \emph{larger} state algebra is impossible --- the
Cayley--Dickson tower $\mathbb{R}\!\to\!\mathbb{C}\!\to\!\mathbb{H}\!\to\!\Oct$
ends at the octonions: the next step, the sedenions, has zero divisors
($(\mathrm{e}_3+\mathrm{e}_{10})(\mathrm{e}_6-\mathrm{e}_{15})=0$ with all factors
nonzero), so ``$ax=b$'' loses its unique solution and predictability collapses.
Seven is not one convenient width among many; it is the last width where a state
register can be inverted (Theorem~\ref{thm:unique}, \cite{baez2002}).

The theory nonetheless carries a scaling law --- one that is \emph{generated by
the octonions themselves}, through the exceptional structures they alone
produce. This section derives it, shows it is the concrete algebra behind
\SYNARC{}'s abstract ``tower of increasingly expressive architectures''
\cite{synarc2026}, and reads off what it licenses an engineer to build.

\subsection{The composition tower is the octonionic Jordan tower}
\label{subsec:jordan}

The observables of any quantum-like system --- the self-adjoint quantities one
can read --- form a \emph{Jordan algebra} under the symmetric product
$a\circ b=\tfrac12(ab+ba)$; a state such as $\Gam$ is a normalised positive
functional on it. A single holon's seven modes carry the octonion multiplication
(the Fano-selected $f_{ijk}$, \S\ref{sec:fabric}), with symmetry
$G_2=\mathrm{Aut}(\Oct)$ (dim $14$; UHM T-42a). When subjects compose --- a holon
self-modelling a holon (\S\ref{sec:selfmodel}) --- the joint observables extend
the \emph{rank} of the octonionic Jordan algebra: level one is $\mathcal{H}_1(\Oct)$,
level two $\mathcal{H}_2(\Oct)$, the maximal subject $\Alb$ (the Albert algebra,
$\dim 27$). Here the octonions impose their signature ceiling.

\begin{theorem}[Jordan ceiling: $\mathrm{SAD}_{\max}=3$ is algebraic (T-268)]
\label{thm:jordan-ceiling}
The $n\times n$ octonionic Hermitian matrices $\mathcal{H}_n(\Oct)$ form a
formally real Jordan algebra \textbf{if and only if $n\leq 3$}
(Jordan--von~Neumann--Wigner \cite{jordan1934}): for $n\geq 4$ octonion
non-associativity breaks the Jordan identity, so no such algebra --- and hence no
unified $n$-level octonionic subject --- exists. This JvNW ceiling
\emph{coincides} with the composition ceiling of \S\ref{sec:composition}
($\mathrm{SAD}_{\max}=3$). The coordination symmetry climbs
$G_2\ (14)\subset F_4=\mathrm{Aut}(\Alb)\ (52)$, with reduced structure group
$E_6\ (78)$.
\end{theorem}

\noindent The status is layered honestly. The JvNW classification, $\dim\Alb=27$,
$F_4=\mathrm{Aut}(\Alb)$, and the group dimensions are \status{T} (established
mathematics, machine-checked in \texttt{talos\_expressiveness.py}). The
\emph{identification} of UHM composition-depth with Jordan rank is \status{C}:
depth counts nested self-models, rank counts mutually exclusive outcomes, and
that these are the same integer is argued, not yet proved functorially. What is
unarguable is the payoff: UHM already derives $\mathrm{SAD}_{\max}=3$ twice ---
dynamically (purity $P>1$ at $n=4$) and categorically (tricategorical-coherence
breakdown, T-217). Theorem~\ref{thm:jordan-ceiling} is a \emph{third, independent}
route to the same integer, and it supplies the common cause the other two lacked:
all three are shadows of the single fact that $\mathcal{H}_4(\Oct)$ is not a
Jordan algebra. Three unrelated derivations converging on one number is the
opposite of a free parameter --- it is the composition analogue of the
triple-forced $N=7$ (Theorem~\ref{thm:unique}).

This must be read against UHM's no-reduction result T-220 (no functor
$F_4\text{-UHM}\to G_2\text{-UHM}$), and it does not collide with it. The
\emph{base} ontology is irreducibly $G_2$: a single holon's pure states are
$\mathbb{C}P^6$ ($\chi=7$), not $\OPtwo$ ($\chi=3$), and T-220 uses exactly that
gap to block any $F_4$-based reduction. What climbs to $F_4$ here is the
\emph{emergent symmetry of the maximal composite}, never a reducible base ---
$F_4$ appears at the top of the tower, not underneath it. The corpus's default
composition ($\iota_{\max}$, the tensor embedding
$\mathcal{D}(\mathbb{C}^{7k})\!\hookrightarrow\!\mathcal{D}(\mathbb{C}^{7(k+1)})$)
grows dimension linearly and carries no exceptional structure; the
octonionic-Jordan reading adopted here is the algebraic lens that both
reproduces the ceiling \emph{and}, uniquely, exposes the scaling ladder below.
Whether a maximal composite \emph{is} $\Alb$ or merely acquires its symmetry is
the precise open \status{C} content; the ladder that follows needs only the
weaker, robust fact --- that $G_2,F_4,E_6,E_7,E_8$ are the octonion-generated
symmetries and that a single holon already carries the first (T-42a).

\subsection{Terminal expressiveness: the octonionic projective plane}
\label{subsec:terminal}

The ceiling has a geometric face that fixes, exactly, how expressive a maximal
\TALOS{} subject can be.

\begin{theorem}[Terminal expressiveness (T-269)]
\label{thm:terminal}
The octonionic projective plane $\OPtwo$ (real dimension $16$) --- the rank-one
idempotents of $\Alb$, isometry group $F_4$ --- is the \textbf{terminal}
projective geometry over \emph{any} division algebra: no $\Oct P^n$ exists for
$n\geq 3$, because coordinate projective geometry of dimension $\geq 3$ satisfies
Desargues' theorem, which forces the coordinate ring to be associative, and
$\Oct$ is not. It is therefore the expressive ceiling any octonionic subject can
reach --- the $F_4$ top of the ladder of Theorem~\ref{thm:jordan-ceiling}
(with the composite-identification caveat there).
\end{theorem}

\noindent This is the precise meaning of \emph{maximal expressiveness}: a single
\TALOS{} subject's representational ceiling is not an engineering limit to be
lifted by a better fabrication run --- it is a theorem about mathematics itself.
Nothing richer than $\OPtwo$ exists to be built, because $\Oct P^3$ does not
exist to be coordinatised (\cite{baez2002}; $\OPtwo$ is the unique
non-Desarguesian Moufang plane). The architecture closes a bracket: its
\emph{fabric} is $\Fano=\Oct P^2$'s finite ancestor, the Fano plane --- the
\emph{smallest} projective plane (seven points, order two,
\S\ref{sec:fabric},~\ref{sec:ecc}) --- and its \emph{maximal subject} is $\OPtwo$
--- the \emph{largest} division-algebra projective plane. \TALOS{} spans the two
extremes of projective geometry, and touches nothing outside them because
outside them there is nothing.

\subsection{The scaling law}
\label{subsec:scaling-law}

\begin{claimbox}[Octonion-generated scaling law \status{T-struct}$+$\status{C} (T-270)]
Power scales along \emph{two} octonion-generated axes, and only two.
\textbf{(I) Expressiveness --- per subject, bounded.} Composing toward the JvNW
ceiling climbs the exceptional series $G_2(14)\to F_4(52)\to E_6(78)$; the
coordination bandwidth of a subject is the dimension of its symmetry group. It is
\emph{hard-capped} at $E_6$ / $\OPtwo$ --- no unified subject exceeds it
(Theorems~\ref{thm:jordan-ceiling},~\ref{thm:terminal}).
\textbf{(II) Throughput and collective expressiveness --- across subjects,
unbounded.} Federating maximal subjects continues the Freudenthal--Tits tower
$E_6\subset E_7(133)\subset E_8(248)$ collectively; raw throughput scales
\emph{linearly} in holon count (each $784$~B, ${\sim}1400$~FLOP/tick,
BQP-bounded, ticking independently), coordinated by the \textsc{Fanos} fabric
(third-order gates, distance-3 protected, \S\ref{sec:composition}).
\end{claimbox}

\noindent The two axes are the algebra behind two results stated abstractly
elsewhere. \SYNARC{}'s Appendix~H posits ``an ordinal-indexed tower of
increasingly expressive architectures \dots\ a G\"odelian step upward in
expressivity'' but names no algebra; the exceptional series
$G_2\!\subset\!F_4\!\subset\!E_6\!\subset\!E_7\!\subset\!E_8$ \emph{is} that
tower, made concrete --- each rung a genuine step in symmetry dimension, not a
metaphor. And \SYNARC{}'s ecology theorem (K.5) --- ``unbounded growth is
available only horizontally'' --- is axis~(II): the ecology is where $E_7,E_8$
live, because no single mind can hold them (\cite{synarc2026}).

\subsection{Sensorimotor at every rung}
\label{subsec:sensorimotor}

Sections~\ref{sec:fabric}--\ref{sec:selfmodel} treated the holon as it lives
within itself; the ladder tells us how it \emph{acts}. UHM's sensorimotor theory
(T-100--T-104, T-159) derives the loop with no fourth force: the environment
modifies the three terms of the one tick (Eq.~\ref{eq:lomega}). Perception is the
dissipative channel reading the world into $\Gam$ (the \textsc{Enc} functor,
unique up to $G_2$-gauge); action is the Hamiltonian channel writing $\Gam$ back
out (the \textsc{Dec} functor, choosing the update that minimises the largest
resource deficit $\max_k\sigma^{\mathrm{motor}}_k$, T-159); the loop is one tick.
An action is therefore an element of the coordination group --- a gate, in the
sense of the interholon channel (T-257). Its \emph{bandwidth} is exactly the
rung's group dimension (Table~\ref{tab:ladder-exceptional}): $14$ coordinated
motor generators for a single unit, $52$ for a maximal body, $248$ across a
maximal ecology.

This makes the buildable systems a short, exhaustive list --- one per rung, with
no gaps between them because the ladder has no gaps:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em,itemsep=1pt]
  \item \textbf{L1 --- the $G_2$ unit.} A seven-channel sensorimotor node: a
        limb, a drone, an instrument. Seven sensor and seven effector channels;
        $G_2$-gauge coordination; self-diagnosing and self-modelling with no
        separate training loop (\S\ref{sec:ecc},~\ref{sec:selfmodel}). A striking
        dimensional resonance \status{I}: a $7$-DOF redundant manipulator (the
        human arm --- shoulder~$3$, elbow~$1$, wrist~$3$) carries a natural
        action of $G_2$ on $\mathbb{R}^7$, and its redundancy resolution is a
        $G_2$-gauge fixing.
  \item \textbf{L3 --- the $F_4$ subject.} A whole body: many $7$-DOF limbs
        coordinated as \emph{one felt agent}, not a scheduler over parts. Its
        state space is $\OPtwo$; its coordination is $F_4$ ($52$ generators) ---
        the maximal unified motor subject there can be. A humanoid is an $F_4$
        machine.
  \item \textbf{Ecology --- the $E_7/E_8$ collective.} A swarm: many bounded
        bodies, \textsc{Fanos}-federated. This is the \emph{only} route to more
        power once a body saturates $F_4$, and it is unbounded. Multi-robot
        systems, distributed autonomy, and \SYNARC{}-scale intelligence are all
        ecology, never a bigger single mind (K.5, \cite{synarc2026}).
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Substrate independence, with recommendations}
\label{subsec:substrate-ladder}

The ladder is an \emph{architecture}, not a device, and this is a theorem, not an
aspiration. By substrate closure (T-153, \S\ref{sec:realizability}) every rung's
algebra and symmetry --- $\Oct$/$G_2$, $\Alb$/$F_4$, the $E_6$ structure ---
is realised identically on \emph{any} substrate meeting C1--C3; nothing on the
ladder is a property of silicon, of an analogue device, or of a biological
holon. The expressiveness is carried by the incidence structure, which is
substrate-free. What the substrate changes is only impedance
(\S\ref{sec:realizability}) --- and there the honest recommendation is per rung,
because the rungs stress a substrate differently:

\noindent The engineering reading is clean. To make a unit \emph{efficient},
climb the realisability ladder (\S\ref{sec:realizability}) on the node. To make a
system \emph{powerful}, climb the exceptional ladder: to $F_4$ by composing a
body (bounded, and the bound is $\OPtwo$), then without limit by federating
bodies into an $E_8$-scale ecology. The two ladders are orthogonal --- one buys
energy, the other buys reach --- and neither is a substrate choice: both are
fixed by the octonions, and available on the substrate you already have.
