\section{ABI details: layout and tick pseudocode}
\label{app:abi}

\paragraph{State layout.} A holon is a contiguous block; a subject is a
four-holon tower (levels $0$--$3$, \S\ref{sec:composition}).
\begin{center}\small
\begin{tabular}{@{}rll@{}}
\toprule
offset & size & field \\
\midrule
$0$    & $784$ & \texttt{gamma} : \DM{} ($49$ complex \texttt{f64}, LE) --- level 0 \\
$784$  & $784$ & level 1 \DM{} \\
$1568$ & $784$ & level 2 \DM{} \\
$2352$ & $784$ & level 3 \DM{} \\
\midrule
       & $3136$ & total subject state (fits in L1) \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{center}
This is byte-identical to the \SYNARC{} \texttt{gamma}/\texttt{rho\_ep} layout
(v2.2 canon): a \SYNARC{} checkpoint is a \TALOS{} state image and vice
versa, with no marshalling.

\paragraph{Tick (per level).}
\begin{center}\small
\begin{tabular}{@{}ll@{}}
\toprule
\multicolumn{2}{@{}l}{\texttt{tick($\Gam$, $\Delta\tau$):}} \\
\quad\texttt{$\Gam \leftarrow$ U\_half($\Gam$, $\Delta\tau/2$)} & // reversible, Landauer-free (T-262) \\
\quad\texttt{$s \leftarrow$ syndrome($\Gam$)} & // free byproduct (line closure, Prin.~\ref{prin:selfdiag}) \\
\quad\texttt{$\Gam \leftarrow$ dissip($\Gam$, $\Delta\tau$)} & // Fano-sparse; exports entropy (forget) \\
\quad\texttt{$\Gam \leftarrow$ regen($\Gam$, $\rhostar$)} & // BKM step to self-model (learn = refresh) \\
\quad\texttt{$\Gam \leftarrow$ U\_half($\Gam$, $\Delta\tau/2$)} & \\
\quad\texttt{assert exact\_laws($\Gam$)} & // $\Phi{=}7P{-}1$, $\Tr{=}1$, PSD; $s{=}0$ if healthy \\
\quad\texttt{return ($\Gam$, $s$)} & \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{center}
The returned syndrome $s$ is the DIAKRISIS input on the \SYNARC{} side; a
non-zero $s$ localises a degraded sector (Appendix~\ref{app:fano}) with no
extra pass. Update magnitude in \texttt{regen} is clamped to
$r_{\mathrm{stab}}=\sqrt{P-2/7}$; the fraction of the tick budget spent in
\texttt{regen} is held $\ge\Rth=1/3$.
